Japanese Education Statistics Dataset

I maintain a reposity on Github which contains an Educational Dataset for Japan (2005-2022) and an explanation of the variables in it. The dataset includes metrics such as the number of junior colleges and universities per 100,000 persons, entrance capacity indexes for junior colleges and universities, ratios of students in national, public, and private institutions, per-student education expenditures across different school types.

The data covers all education levels, from kindergarten to college, and includes all prefectures in Japan. It allows for regional and temporal analysis of trends in educational policy, resource allocation, and institutional capacity.
