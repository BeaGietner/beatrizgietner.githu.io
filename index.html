About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at UCD, specializing in Education and Labour Economics. My research focuses on the intersection of human capital and economic growth. I like to think about models and how to better translate lived experiences into mathematical forms. I do most of my data analysis in R, and I have a thing for charts, plots, and diagrams.
