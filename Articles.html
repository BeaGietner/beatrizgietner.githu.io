Master's dissertation

You can download my Master's dissertation here. I wrote about how the GFC altered Leaving Cert (the Irish national entrance exam to third-level education) candidates' choice of majors.

I found a significant gender gap in STEM subjects and varied trends in student participation. There was an increase in students taking Agricultural Science, Applied Mathematics, Biology, and Chemistry, but a decrease in those taking Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, and Physics and Chemistry. Additionally, admission points for STEM-related college courses, especially in Healthcare, Chemistry, Nanotechnology, Biology, and Computer Science, saw a notable rise.
